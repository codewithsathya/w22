CHIEF GUEST

V K Aatre

“There are no limits to what you can accomplish when you know who you are and have faith in what you can do”

  • This very fact is symbolised by Dr.Vasudev Kalkunte Aatre, an Indian scientist and former head of the Defence Research and Development Organisation (DRDO) and a recipient of Padma Bhushan in the year 2000.
  • He was also bestowed with Padma Vibhushan award in the year 2016.
  • Wissenaire’21 kickstarts this edition with the honor of having Dr.V.K.Aatre as the Chief Guest for the inaugural evening.
